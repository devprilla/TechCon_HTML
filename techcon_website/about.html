History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and entrepreneurs. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential technology conferences globally, featuring cutting-edge innovations and industry-defining talks.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech industry. Our mission is to connect brilliant minds and spark transformative ideas that shape the future of technology.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Jane Smith - A leading AI researcher who shared groundbreaking insights on ethical machine learning at TechCon 2021.

  • Carlos Nguyen - TechCon 2019 keynote speaker and founder of CloudWare, a platform revolutionizing data storage.

  • Fatima Diallo - Spoke at TechCon 2022 on global cybersecurity trends and resilience strategies for enterprises.
